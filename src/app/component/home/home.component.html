Features

MANAGE BOOKINGS ON THE GO!

ORGANISER

Say goodbye to pen and paper. Allow your staff to check their day-to-day scheduling, so they can be prepared for the day ahead.


NOTIFICATION ALERTS

You will receive notifications for all the bookings made your customers through your own branded App.


APPOINTMENTS

With a simple tap on the appointment at the home page, scheduler or calendar, you can see all the information regarding current and upcoming appointments.


OTP SIGN UP BY YOUR CUSTOMERS

Your booking App will connect genuine clients by entering OTP when signing up (One Time Passcode, will be sent to their mobile number to verify), where falls bookings are highly avoided and customers can pay online (It is an optional feature for salons)..
